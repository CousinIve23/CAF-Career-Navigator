BMQ — Basic Training
QMB — Instruction de base
What to expect at Basic Military Qualification
Ce a quoi vous attendre a la Qualification militaire de base

BMQ (Basic Military Qualification) is the entry-level training every CAF member must complete. Regular Force BMQ is at the CAF Leadership and Recruit School (CAFLRS) in Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu, Quebec — approximately 10 weeks. Reserve BMQ is typically done on weekends and/or a full-time summer block.

La QMB (Qualification militaire de base) est la formation initiale que chaque membre des FAC doit completer. La QMB de la Force reguliere se deroule a l'Ecole de leadership et de recrues des FAC (ELRFC) a Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu — environ 10 semaines. La QMB de la Reserve se fait generalement les fins de semaine ou lors d'un bloc estival.

  • The first 2–3 weeks are the hardest — sleep deprivation, physical exhaustion, and homesickness are normal and by design
  • Phone access is heavily restricted early on. Tell your family in advance.
  • You will be issued uniforms, equipment, and a service number
  • Training covers drill, weapons handling, first aid, field craft, CAF values, and fitness
  • You will complete the FORCE test (fitness standard)
  • Les 2–3 premieres semaines sont les plus difficiles — manque de sommeil, epuisement et ennui du foyer sont normaux
  • L'acces au telephone est tres limite au debut. Prevenez votre famille.
  • Vous recevrez uniformes, equipement et numero de service
  • Formation : exercice militaire, armes, premiers soins, campagne, valeurs des FAC, condition physique
  • Vous completerez le test FORCE

The FORCE test is the universal fitness standard. 4 components:

  • Sandbag Lift — lift 20 kg to 1 metre, repeatedly
  • Intermittent Loaded Shuttles — carry 20 kg over 20 m with rest intervals
  • 20-Metre Rushes — sprint, drop to prone, get up, repeat
  • Sandbag Drag — drag 20 kg over 20 m without stopping

The minimum standard is achievable with basic fitness. You retake the FORCE test annually.

Le test FORCE est la norme de condition physique universelle. 4 composantes :

  • Lever de sacs de sable — soulever 20 kg a 1 m, de facon repetee
  • Navettes avec charge — transporter 20 kg sur 20 m avec intervalles
  • Courses de 20 m — sprinter, se coucher, se relever, repeter
  • Trainee de sac de sable — trainer 20 kg sur 20 m sans arret

La norme minimale est atteignable avec une condition physique de base. Vous repasserez le test annuellement.

An injury does not mean automatic release. You'll be assessed at base medical. If you need recovery time, you may be placed on Duty with Limitations (DwL) — a temporary status restricting certain physical activities. For extended recovery, you may move to a rehab/holding platoon. Medical Employment Limitations (MELs) document what duties you can perform. Do not hide injuries to keep pace — this makes things worse. Most members recover and complete their course.

Une blessure ne signifie pas une liberation automatique. Vous serez evalue a la clinique medicale. Si vous avez besoin de recuperer, vous pourriez etre place en Service avec restrictions (SAS). Pour une recuperation prolongee, vous pourriez etre transfere a un peloton de rehabilitation. Les limitations d'emploi medical (LEM) documentent vos restrictions. Ne cachez pas les blessures — cela aggrave les choses.

You are not trapped. Voluntary release during BMQ is possible but strongly discouraged in the first few weeks. If you fail a component, you may be recoursed — sent to redo the failed portion. Talk to your Platoon Warrant before making any decision.

💡Training staff consistently observe: the overwhelming majority of recruits who want to quit in the first 2 weeks are glad they stayed by week 6. Seek support before deciding anything permanent.Les instructeurs observent systematiquement : la grande majorite des recrues qui veulent partir dans les 2 premieres semaines sont contentes d'etre restees a la 6e semaine.

Vous n'etes pas coince. La liberation volontaire pendant la QMB est possible, mais fortement deconseilee dans les premieres semaines. Si vous echouez a un composant, vous pourriez etre remis en cycle. Parlez a votre adjudant de peloton avant toute decision.

Trade Training & First Unit
Formation de metier et premiere unite
After BMQ — learning your job and understanding the rank structure
Apres la QMB — apprendre votre metier et comprendre la structure des grades

Trade training. You'll be posted to a training establishment for your occupation-specific course (DP1).

  • Duration varies: Combat arms 3–4 months. Technical trades 6–12+ months. You are paid throughout.
  • Location: CFB Borden (support/tech), CFB Gagetown (Army combat), CFB Esquimalt/Halifax (Navy), others.
  • PAT platoon: You may wait weeks or months for your course. Use the time — get fit, study, volunteer.
  • After trade training: Posted to your first operational unit for on-the-job training (OJT).

Formation de metier. Vous serez mute a un etablissement de formation pour votre cours specifique (PD1).

  • Duree variable : Armes de combat 3–4 mois. Metiers techniques 6–12+ mois. Vous etes paye.
  • Lieu : BFC Borden, BFC Gagetown, BFC Esquimalt/Halifax, et autres.
  • Peloton PAT : Attente possible de semaines ou mois. Utilisez le temps — condition physique, etude, benevolat.
  • Apres la formation : Mutation a votre premiere unite operationnelle pour formation en cours d'emploi.

NCM ranks: Private → Corporal (~4 yrs) → Master Corporal → Sergeant → Warrant Officer → MWO → CWO

Officer ranks: Officer Cadet → 2Lt → Lt → Capt → Maj → LCol → Col → General ranks

Pte to Cpl is largely time-based (~4 years). Above Corporal, promotions are merit-based through selection boards. Your annual PERs matter enormously.

Grades MR : Soldat → Caporal (~4 ans) → Caporal-chef → Sergent → Adjudant → Adjudant-maitre → Adjudant-chef

Grades d'officier : Eleve-officier → Slt → Lt → Capt → Maj → Lcol → Col → Grades de general

De soldat a caporal, c'est principalement base sur le temps. Au-dessus, les promotions sont au merite par des comites de selection. Vos RER comptent enormement.

SISIP — Your Financial Protection
RARM — Votre protection financiere
The benefit nobody explains — in plain English
L'avantage que personne n'explique — en francais clair

SISIP is your military financial safety net. Two parts: Long Term Disability (LTD) — income replacement if you can't work — and SISIP Financial — planning and insurance services designed for CAF members.

Le RARM est votre filet de securite financiere militaire. Deux parties : l'Assurance-invalidite de longue duree (AILD) — remplacement de revenu — et les Services financiers du RARM — planification et assurances pour les membres des FAC.

Regular Force: Yes — automatic from enrolment. Check your pay statement for the CFLTD premium.

Reserve Force: Class C and Class B over 180 days: automatic. Class A and shorter Class B: can purchase optional coverage.

Force reguliere : Oui — automatique des l'enrolement. Verifiez la prime AILD sur votre releve de paie.

Force de reserve : Classe C et classe B de plus de 180 jours : automatique. Classe A et classe B courte : couverture facultative disponible.

If totally disabled, SISIP LTD pays 75% of your pre-release salary until age 65. Applies whether injured on duty or off duty. Administered by Manulife: 1-800-267-6681.

En cas d'invalidite totale, l'AILD du RARM paie 75 % de votre salaire avant liberation jusqu'a 65 ans. S'applique en service ou hors service. Administre par Manuvie : 1-800-267-6681.

  • Confirm your CFLTD premium appears on your pay statement
  • Book a free financial planning session with SISIP Financial
  • Keep records of any injuries or medical issues from the start
  • Confirmez que la prime AILD apparait sur votre releve de paie
  • Reservez une seance gratuite de planification financiere avec les Services financiers du RARM
  • Conservez des dossiers de toute blessure ou probleme medical des le debut

Career Progression & Courses
Progression de carriere et cours
PLQ, PERs, Career Managers, and the path to promotion
PLQ, RER, gestionnaires de carriere et le chemin vers la promotion

Promotion requires both trade qualifications and leadership qualifications:

  • PLQ (Primary Leadership Qualification): Required for promotion to Sergeant. Competitive, leadership-focused.
  • ILQ (Intermediate Leadership Qualification): Required for Warrant Officer.
  • DP courses: DP1 after BMQ (trade qualification), DP2/DP3 for advanced trade progression.

Without the required courses, you cannot be promoted regardless of time served. Course seats are competitive — apply early and express your intent to your Career Manager.

La promotion exige des qualifications de metier et de leadership :

  • PLQ (Qualification elementaire en leadership) : Requis pour la promotion au grade de sergent.
  • ILQ (Qualification intermediaire en leadership) : Requis pour l'adjudant.
  • Cours PD : PD1 apres la QMB, PD2/PD3 pour le perfectionnement.

Sans les cours requis, vous ne pouvez pas etre promu. Les places sont concurrentielles — postulez tot.

PERs (Performance Evaluation Reports) are your annual performance record. Above Corporal, they are the primary factor in competitive promotion boards. A strong PER can get you promoted ahead of schedule. A weak PER can hold you back for years.

💡Read your PER before signing. You have the right to add comments. Ask your supervisor what they're looking for before the assessment period ends.Lisez votre RER avant de le signer. Vous avez le droit d'ajouter des commentaires. Demandez a votre superviseur ce qu'il recherche.

Les RER (Rapports d'evaluation du rendement) sont votre dossier annuel de rendement. Au-dessus du caporal, ils sont le principal facteur pour les comites de selection. Un excellent RER peut accelerer votre promotion. Un RER mediocre peut vous retenir pendant des annees.

Career Managers (CMs) at NDHQ Ottawa match members to positions and manage the posting process. Many members never reach out — this is a mistake. Your CM can explain available positions, flag your preferences, tell you what courses you need, and give honest career advice.

How to contact: Through your unit admin or the My Career portal (EMAA/SGPM system). Discuss: career goals, geographic preferences, upcoming postings, desired courses.

Les gestionnaires de carriere (GC) au QGDN d'Ottawa font correspondre les membres aux postes. Beaucoup de membres ne les contactent jamais — c'est une erreur. Votre GC peut expliquer les postes disponibles, noter vos preferences et donner des conseils de carriere.

Comment contacter : Par la section d'administration de votre unite ou le portail Ma carriere (systeme SGPM). A discuter : objectifs de carriere, preferences geographiques, mutations a venir, cours souhaites.

The CF memo is the standard internal communication tool. Used for: leave requests, grievances, complaints, administrative matters. Format: Date, To:, From:, Subject:, numbered paragraphs, signature block. One subject per memo. Cite the relevant policy. Be clear and professional. Your admin section has the template.

Le memo des FC est l'outil de communication interne standard. Utilise pour : demandes de conge, griefs, plaintes, questions administratives. Format : Date, A :, De :, Objet :, paragraphes numerotes, bloc de signature. Un seul sujet par memo. Citez la politique pertinente. Votre section d'administration a le modele.

Second Language Profile (SLP): Rated A (basic), B (intermediate), C (advanced) in reading, writing, and oral. Bilingual Bonus: Monthly allowance for meeting bilingual position requirements. Above Sergeant/Captain, many HQ and senior positions require a bilingual profile. The CAF offers free Second Language Education (SLE) during work hours.

💡Start your second language training early. Getting your profile established before you need it for promotion is far better than scrambling when the opportunity arrives.Commencez votre formation en langue seconde tot. Obtenir votre profil avant d'en avoir besoin est bien mieux que de vous demener au dernier moment.

Profil de la seconde langue (PSL) : Note A (base), B (intermediaire), C (avance) en lecture, ecriture et oral. Prime bilingue : Allocation mensuelle. Au-dessus du sergent/capitaine, de nombreux postes exigent un profil bilingue. Les FAC offrent la Formation linguistique en langue seconde (FLLS) gratuite pendant les heures de travail.

Postings & the Posting Process
Mutations et processus de mutation
How postings work, mobility allowance, and service couple co-location
Comment les mutations fonctionnent, indemnite de mobilite et co-localisation

The Mobility Allowance replaces the old Posting Allowance effective April 1, 2026:

  • Moves 1–3: $13,500 per move
  • Moves 4–6: $20,250 per move
  • Beyond 6: $27,000 per move

L'indemnite de mobilite remplace l'ancienne indemnite de mutation depuis le 1er avril 2026 :

  • Mutations 1–3 : 13 500 $
  • Mutations 4–6 : 20 250 $

The Canadian Forces Housing Differential (CFHD) helps members posted to expensive cities. Calculated based on the difference between local rental costs and a percentage of your pay. Updated every July. Lower-ranked members in expensive cities get proportionally more help.
