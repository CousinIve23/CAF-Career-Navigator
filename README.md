# CAF Career Navigator

**Free bilingual career companion for Canadian Armed Forces members — from first application through mid-career to transition out.**

**Live tool:** [cousinive23.github.io/CAF-Career-Navigator](https://cousinive23.github.io/CAF-Career-Navigator/)

Built by a CAF community member. Powered by Claude AI. Not an official CAF product.

---

## What Is This?

A single-file HTML tool that helps CAF members and applicants understand what happens next at every career stage. No login. No tracking. No ads. Just answers.

**67 bilingual FAQs** covering:
- Application process, eligibility, timelines
- BMQ — what to expect, week by week
- Pay, allowances, and the benefits nobody tells you about
- Career progression — PERs, PLQ, Career Managers
- Postings, IR, service couples, buy vs. rent
- Deployments — what to expect, family support, tax-free income
- MFRC, PSP, education funding, childcare
- Military justice — Code of Service Discipline
- Career changes — OT/VOT, component transfers, CFR
- Leaving the CAF — release types, pension, VAC benefits

**Plus interactive features:**
- Career stage onboarding — personalized content based on where you are
- 6 interactive checklists (Pre-Application, Pre-BMQ, First 30 Days, Posting Received, Pre-Deployment, Voluntary Release)
- Real-time search across all 67 FAQs
- Bookmark system — save FAQs you need to find again
- 45 rotating "Did You Know?" tips
- Quick Contacts card — every key service explained in plain language
- Share any FAQ with a direct link
- Works offline (Service Worker)
- Dark mode
- Print-friendly

Available in **English and French** with proper CAF military terminology.

---

## How to Use

1. Open `CAF Career Navigator 🍁.html` in any browser
2. Select your career stage (Thinking about joining → Transitioning out)
3. Browse FAQs, use checklists, search for what you need

No installation. No internet required after first load. Works on phone, tablet, and desktop.
